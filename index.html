About Me
From a young age I tried to do everything. When I was 5 I wanted to be a race car driver, doctor, dog walker, professional basketball player and scientist, all simultaneously of course. Since then, I'd like to think that my interests have changed, and I've grown a foot or two, but my high aspirations have not.

Aerospace is my main passion. From joining the University of Toronto Rocketry Team in grade 10, running the Aerospace club at my school, to building a high altitude balloon, and much more, I have built some cool projects and learned a lot along the way. In the future, I want to use my skills and experience in aerospace to help advance humanity, and continue our exploration of the stars! 🚀

Entrepreneurship is my second interest. I like to meet new people, and grow ideas with a positive social impact. From travelling to silicon valley and meeting people at google and facebook, to helping run North America's largest annual student-run current affairs conference, and now working on my latest venture Wavefrnt.
